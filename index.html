Welcome to the Widsið translation and thesis

This site exists to showcase the work done by Sarah Monnier to satisfy the requirements for her Master of English Language and Literature degree from Signum University.

Click on the Widsið dropdown menu to explore the various views of Widsið and glossary tools as well as an example alignment of Beowulf to display the rapid extensibility potential of this project.

Select the Thesis dropdown menu to read the paper about this project, explaining more about the poem itself, why it was chosen for this project, and what the potential future for this sort of work is.
