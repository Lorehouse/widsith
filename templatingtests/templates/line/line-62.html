Translation


With the Saxons I was and the Sycgans and with the Suardones.

Named Entities

  • Seaxan: The original Saxons of Germany
  • Sycga: A tribe only otherwise known from the 'Secgena' of the Finnsburh Fragment l24.
  • Sweordwere: From Tacitus, as Malone so adeptly argues in 202-203.
