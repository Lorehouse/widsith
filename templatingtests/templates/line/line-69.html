Translation


With the Rugians I was and with the Glomman and with the Romans.

Named Entities

  • Rugas: These are the same people as the Holmrycgas of line 21, known to history as the Rugii or the Rugians. Wikipedia
  • Glomman: A tribe perhaps the same as Tacitus's Lemovii on the Oder estuary (tgn 1228010, http://vocab.getty.edu/page/tgn/1128010).
  • Rumwalas: Malone (193) identifies the Rumwalas as the eastern Roman empire, which is heavily disputed by other authors @@@ Check Weiskott
