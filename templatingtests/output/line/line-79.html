mid Scottum ic wæs ond mid Peohtum ond mid Scridefinnum;
mid Scottas ic wesan ond mid Peohtas ond mid Scridefinnas
prep dpn pn psv conj prep dpn conj prep dpn

Previous Next

Translation


With the Irish I was and with the Picts and with the Lapps.

Named Entities

  • Scottas: Not the Scots, actually the Irish.
  • Peohtas: The Picts of history. Wikipedia
  • Scridefinnas: The Lapps of arctic Norway, Scride- (skis) exists to differentiate them from the Finns proper.
