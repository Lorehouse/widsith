Meaca Myrgings, Mearchealf the Hundings.

Named Entities

Meaca: Unknown king of the Myrgings

Mearchealf: unknowng king of the Hundings, but his name may be derived from mearh 'horse' and ealf 'elf'.

Hundingas: nickname for the Langobards
