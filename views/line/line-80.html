Translation


With the Lidwiccas I was and with the Leonas and with the Langobards,

Named Entities

Lidwicingas: Malone emends this to Lidingum and associates them with the Liðungar of Islenzkir Analar, but others associate it as is with the Lidwiccas in the OE Annals. I have chosen to leave it unchanged for the moment

Leonas: Malone identifies these as the Leuoni of Ptolemy, a tribe located in central Skandia. (Malone 180)
