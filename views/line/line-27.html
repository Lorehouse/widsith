Line 27

Fin Folcwalding Fresna cynne.
Fin Folcwalding Frēsan cynn
nsn nsn asn isn
Fin son of Folcwalda the Frisian tribe
Previous Next

Translation


Fin Folkwalding the Frisian tribe.

Named Entities

  • Fin: King of the Frisians and the Finn from Beowulf and the Finnsburuh Fragment.
  • Folcwalding: The patronymic of Finn's father, but Chambers suggests that it was a title that alliterated conveniently rather than Finn's father's real name (1921, 200)
  • Frēsan: These are the Frisians of history. Wikipedia
