Translation


Fin Folkwalding the Frisian tribe.

Named Entities

Fin: King of rhe Frisians and the Finn from Beowulf and the Finnsburuh Fragment

Folcwalding: The patronymic of Finn's father, but Chambers suggests that it was a title that alliterated conveniently rather than Finn's father's real name (1921, 200)

Frēsan: These are the Frisians of history. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frisians
