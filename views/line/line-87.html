Translation


and with the Ilwan and with the Istas and Idumings.

Named Entities

  • Ilwan: The Elvaeones of Ptolemy, the Helvecones of Tacitus
  • Iste: A specific tribe of the Ostrogoths
  • Idumingas: Ydumaci of Henricus Lettus, a Livonian tribe.
