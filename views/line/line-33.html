Translation


Hunn the Haetwere, and Holen the Wrosnan.

Named Entities

Hun: This is a king of the Hætwere who appears also in an Icelandic thula and as the champion Hunn who fought in the Battle of Bravellir.

Hætwere: These are the Hetware of Beowulf.

Holen: Malone takes Holen to mean 'protector' in l80 of the Exeter Book Gnomic Verses. Others associate it with OE holegn meaning 'holly'. (Malone 173)

Wrosnan: A Danish island tribe
