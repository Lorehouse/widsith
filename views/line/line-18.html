Translation


Atilla ruled the Huns, Ermanric the Goths,

Named Entities

Ætla: the famous Hun.

Hunas: These are the Huns of history, a nomadic people: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Huns

Gotan: Malone views these as the Ostrogoths, but this is disputed and he may have ruled a broad range of the Gothic peoples. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goths
