Translation


Wulfhere I sought and Wyrmhere, full often war was not at rest there

Named Entities

Wulfhere: One of two princes of the Hræde who let their people in a war against the Huns (Malone 213).

Wyrmhere: The Ormarr of the Hervararsaga, also the Olimarus of Saxo's 5th book. One of two princes of the Hræde who let their people in a war against the Huns (Malone 214).
