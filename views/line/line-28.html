PreviousNext

Translation


Sighere ruled the Sea-Danes the longest,

Named Entities

  • Sigehere: King of the Sea-Danes, the Sigarr of the Snorra Edda

Named Entities

  • Sæ-dene: It is uncertain whether the prefix 'Sæ' means to restrict these to only the Danes of the islands or as an epithet to emphasize the dominance of the Danes on the sea, but either way these are simply the Danes.
