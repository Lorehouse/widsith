Line 58

mid Sweom ond mid Geatum ond mid Suþ-denum.
mid Sweon ond mid Gēatas ond mid Suþ-dene
prep dpn conj prep dpn conj prep dpn
with Swedes and with the Geats and with the south-Danes
Previous Next

Translation


with the Swedes and with the Geats and with the South-Danes.

Named Entities

  • Gēatas: The Geats are common knowledge
  • Suþ-dene: This simply means the South-Danes. In Beowulf, there are North-, East-. West-, and South-Danes, but the Widsith poet only distinguishes the South-Danes (Malone 136, 201).
