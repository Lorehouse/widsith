Translation


With the Gibids I was and with the Winedas and with the Gefflegans.

Named Entities

  • Gefþan: A tribe of the Gothic family mentioned in Beowulf and the Gepids of history. Wikipedia
  • Winedas: The Wends, a generic German name for the Slavs.
  • Gefflēgan: The Aviones of Tacitus but location unknown
